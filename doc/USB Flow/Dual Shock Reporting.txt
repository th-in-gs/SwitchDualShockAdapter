How long does it take to receive one report form the Dual Shock?

One report is at most 21 bytes - or 168 bits.

At 200KHz that's 5e-6s per bit, so 8.4E-4s per transfer - let's double that for tome between bytes = 1.68ms per transfer. That's more than the time between USB interrupts (so we can't poll in-line and expect to send a response in the next interrupt).

At the moment, we send our response starting at the next interrupt after a request from the Switch.
